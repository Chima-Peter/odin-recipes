Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This fast-track recipe for Quick & Easy Spaghetti Bolognese makes a meat sauce that’s bursting with Italian flavor and cooks in just 30 minutes!

Serve this easy meal with a warm loaf of Garlic Bread for the perfect Italian meal everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • Whole Plum Tomatoes
  • Ground Beef Ground beef that’s 80 – 85% lean

ST=teps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until lightly golden, 2 to 3 minutes. Add the garlic and fry for another minute.
  2. Add the ground beef
  3. Fry until fully cooked and no pink shows at all in the meat. Then add the tomato paste and mix it with the meat. Add the oregano.
  4. Add the tomatoes, parsley, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve with freshly cooked spaghetti, and enjoy every last bite!
This dish is a real crowd-pleaser and is loved by adults and kids alike!
